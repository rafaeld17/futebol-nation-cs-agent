# Futebol Nation Help Center — FAQ & Policies

> Futebol Nation sells official and replica World Cup soccer jerseys, kits, and accessories.
> This document is the **single source of truth** for the Super Sub support agent.
> The agent may only state policies that appear here. If a question isn't covered,
> the agent says so and escalates — it never invents a policy.

## Account & ordering

### How do I create an account?
Click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner of futebolnation.com and follow the steps. You can also
check out as a guest without an account — you'll still get order and tracking emails.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are
processed over encrypted, PCI-compliant connections.

### Are my personal and payment details secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) and PCI-DSS-compliant payment processing. Futebol Nation
never stores full card numbers on our servers.

### Can I order by phone?
No. Futebol Nation is online-only; all orders are placed at futebolnation.com. Our support team can help you
troubleshoot checkout, but cannot take payment over the phone.

## Shipping & delivery

### How long does shipping take?
- **Standard:** 3–5 business days (US).
- **Express:** 1–2 business days (US).
- Processing adds 1 business day before a non-personalized order ships.
- **Personalized jerseys** (custom name/number) add **2–3 business days** of production time
  before shipping.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, to most countries, including Brazil, the UK, Canada, and Germany. Costs and delivery
estimates are calculated at checkout based on your destination. International delivery is
typically **6–12 business days** after processing.

### Will I pay customs or import duties on international orders?
Possibly. International orders may be subject to **customs duties and import taxes** set by the
destination country. These are **the customer's responsibility** and are **not** included in
the Futebol Nation price or shipping fee. Futebol Nation cannot calculate or waive them.

### World Cup shipping cutoffs
To receive an order **before the tournament kickoff**, place it by these cutoffs:
- **Standard (US):** at least **7 business days** before you need it.
- **Express (US):** at least **3 business days** before you need it.
- **Personalized jerseys:** add **2–3 business days** to the above for production.
- **International:** at least **14 business days** before you need it.
Orders placed after these windows may not arrive in time, especially during peak World Cup volume.

### How can I track my order?
Log in and open **Order History**, or use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.
You'll need your order number (format `#1000`+) and the email used at checkout.

### Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
Only if the order **has not shipped yet**. Contact support with your order number as soon as
possible and we'll try to update it. Once shipped, the address cannot be changed.

## Jersey sizing, fit & customization

### How do Futebol Nation jerseys fit / what size should I order?
Jerseys run **true to size** in a standard athletic cut. We carry **Youth (S–XL)** and
**Adult (S–3XL)**. **Authentic (player-version)** jerseys have a slimmer, tighter match-fit —
size up if you prefer a relaxed fit. A full size chart with chest/length measurements is on
every product page under **Size Guide**.

### What's the difference between Authentic and Replica jerseys?
- **Authentic / Player Version:** slim match-fit, heat-applied badges, performance fabric. Premium price.
- **Replica / Stadium Version:** relaxed fan fit, stitched/printed badges, comfort fabric. Lower price.

### Can I add a player name and number (customization)?
Yes. On the product page choose **Customize** and enter a name (max 12 characters) and number
(1–99). Official player names/numbers and your own custom text are both supported, subject to
licensing restrictions. Customization adds **2–3 business days** of production time.
**Important:** personalized jerseys are **final sale** — see Returns.

### Are Futebol Nation jerseys authentic / officially licensed?
Yes. Futebol Nation sells officially licensed products and backs every item with an **authenticity
guarantee**. If you believe an item is not authentic, contact support for a full review.

## Returns, refunds & cancellations

### What is your return policy?
Unworn, unwashed items in original condition and packaging (tags attached) can be returned
within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund. Start a return from **Order History → Return**
or contact support. Return shipping for change-of-mind returns is the customer's responsibility.

### Which items can't be returned?
- **Personalized / customized jerseys** (custom name or number) — **final sale, non-returnable**,
  unless the item is defective or we made an error.
- **Final-sale / clearance** items marked as such at purchase.
These exclusions do **not** apply when the item is defective, damaged in transit, or incorrect.

### Can I return an item if I changed my mind?
Yes, within the **30-day** window, if it's unworn and in original condition with tags — except
personalized and final-sale items, which are non-returnable.

### How long do refunds take?
Once we receive and inspect the return, refunds are issued to the original payment method within
**5–7 business days**.

### Can I cancel or change my order?
You can cancel or change an order **only before it ships** (and before production starts for
personalized items). Contact support with your order number right away. Once shipped, use the
returns process instead.

## Problems with an order

### My package is lost, damaged, or hasn't arrived — what do I do?
Contact support with your order number. For damaged or lost-in-transit packages we'll open a
carrier investigation and arrange a **replacement or refund** at no cost to you. (Resolution of
these cases is handled by our support team.)

### I received the wrong item or a defective jersey.
Contact support with your order number and a photo. We'll send the correct/replacement item and
cover return shipping for the incorrect or defective item. (Handled by our support team.)

### A product is sold out / out of stock in my size — can you restock or reserve it?
We can't guarantee restocks or reserve out-of-stock items. Use **Notify Me** on the product page
to get an email if your size comes back in stock. Restock decisions are made by our merchandising
team and handled case-by-case by support.
